What is Ikigai?

The Intersection of Your Divine Gifts

Your Personal Treasure Map

Ikigai (生き甲斐) combines "iki" (life) and "gai" (value). It's the treasure God embedded in your DNA—your unique contribution to the world.

Think of it as your SUPERHERO ORIGIN STORY. Every great hero has unique powers. So do you. Your Ikigai is found at the intersection of four divine gifts:

  • 💖 What you LOVE
    Your heart's deepest desires
  • ⚡ What you're GOOD AT
    Your natural superpowers
  • 💎 What you can be PAID FOR
    Your valuable contribution
  • 🌍 What the world NEEDS
    Your divine mission

✨ Where these four converge, you'll find your Ikigai—
your God-given purpose to impact millions of lives.
